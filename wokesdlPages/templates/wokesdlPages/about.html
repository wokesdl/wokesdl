ABOUT US

Born from the vision of Adamu Malabu and Wedham Laugié, LONG LIVE SDL is more than a brand—it’s an anthem for freedom and fearlessness.

What began as WOKE SEE DA LIGHT, a statement of defiance against societal norms, has grown into an umbrella uniting art, fashion, and music. From pushing boundaries from the streets to bridging sounds between Africa and the world, we are here to make dreams undeniable.

LONG LIVE SDL is a call to community, expression, and breaking free from the limits placed on us. This is not just a brand. It’s a way of life.
