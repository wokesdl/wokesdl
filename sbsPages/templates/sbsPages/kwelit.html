KWELIT

For Carl, music has always been home. Starting out as just a rapper, his frustration with finding the right producer led him to teach himself how to run a home studio—learning production, mixing, and mastering from the ground up. Since his debut in 2021, he’s built his own brand with limited resources, proving his versatility with projects like What You Doing Carl, PAIN, and the latest, MORE PAIN.

Beyond his own music, Carl has produced for artists like [L3Y, etc.], and in 2020, officially became the in-house producer for SoundsBySDL. Now with each new release, he’s shaping an entirely new sonic approach to music.
